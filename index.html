RevGuard — Open-Source Data Assurance Engine ↗

A production-grade Lakehouse framework that assures billing, payments, and revenue data end-to-end: ingest → validate → reconcile → detect anomalies → generate audit evidence. SOX/ICFR financial-data controls as executable pipeline checks, driven by one declarative YAML catalog auditors can read: segregation of duties, approval thresholds, cutoffs, three-way matches, journal balance, and access controls — with auto-generated audit evidence packs and a CI gate that fails the build on high-severity violations.
